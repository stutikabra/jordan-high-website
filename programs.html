PROGRAMS

Explore Our Programs

At Jordan High School, we are committed to providing a well-rounded education that goes beyond academics. Our diverse range of programs in athletics, fine arts, and extracurricular activities are designed to nurture students' talents, foster personal growth, and build a strong sense of community. We encourage all students to get involved and take advantage of the opportunities available to enhance their high school experience.

Athletics

Jordan High School offers a comprehensive and competitive athletics program that provides students with opportunities to participate in a wide range of sports throughout the school year. Our teams emphasize teamwork, discipline, leadership, and sportsmanship while encouraging students to develop both physically and mentally. Through athletics, students build school spirit, form lasting connections with peers, and represent Jordan High School with pride in district and regional competition. Fine Arts

The Fine Arts program at Jordan High School provides students with meaningful opportunities to explore and develop their artistic abilities in a supportive and collaborative environment. With offerings in band and color guard, orchestra, dance, choir, and theater, students are encouraged to build creativity, discipline, and confidence through hands-on learning and performance. Experienced instructors guide students as they refine their skills and participate in performances that extend beyond the classroom, allowing them to engage with both the school and the surrounding community.

Extracurricular Activities

Jordan High School offers more than 50 extracurricular activities that allow students to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build lasting friendships. From academic clubs like Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) and Technology Student Association (TSA) to competitive teams such as Quiz Bowl and VEX Robotics, there is something for everyone. These activities foster leadership, teamwork, and personal growth, enriching the overall high school experience.
