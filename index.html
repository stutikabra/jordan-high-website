About Jordan High School

Established in 2020, Jordan High School is dedicated to academic excellence, student growth, and community leadership. Our campus features state-of-the-art facilities include a modern library, a versatile gymnasium, and a natatorium designed to support both academics and athletics. We strive to provide spaces that inspire learning, creativity, and collaboration.

Message from the Principal

"There are many things in life that can be done over again. High school is not one of those things. You get one opportunity to make the most of it. One opportunity to learn everything you can from your teachers, to play the sport you love, to learn an instrument, take the marching field with your friends, or learn how to make art. These are only a few examples of the opportunities students will have at Jordan. Every day counts and every day at Jordan is the chance to live community, commitment, and resilience."

- Dr. Ethan Crowell, Principal

Jordan Athletics

Our student-athletes thrive in a supportive environment where teamwork, discipline, and excellence come together. From football to swimming to tennis, every student has the opportunity to pursue their passion and reach their potential.

Jordan Fine Arts

Jordan High School's Fine Arts program nurtures creativity, self-expression, and collaboration. Students can explore a variety of disciplines, including band & guard, orchestra, dance, choir, and theater. Our dedicated instructors provide individualized guidance and performance opportunities, preparing students to showcase their talents both on campus and in the community. Through our arts programs, students develop discipline, confidence, and a lifelong appreciation for the arts.

Extracurricular Activities

Jordan High School offers a wide range of extracurricular opportunities. Students can participate in FBLA, robotics, TSA, cultural clubs, and community service clubs. These activities foster leadership, teamwork, and personal growth while helping students connect with the community.
